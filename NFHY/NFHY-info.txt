A sample of monosyllabic binomials from the BNC, annotated for sonority of the two nominals.


Description of the columns:

Binomial – the binomial expression in the more frequent order

OrderA – the frequency of occurrence of the order shown

OrderB – the frequency of occurrence of the order opposite to the one shown

Frozenness – the percentage of the more frequent order

MoreSonorant – which of the two nouns is more sonorant, with the values "first" or "second"
