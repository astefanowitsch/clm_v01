Frequencies of the suffixes _-ic_ and _-ical_ with stems containing the affixes _-olog-_ and _-ist_ in the British National Corpus.


Description of the columns:

Stem – the stem

Affix – the affix, with the values "ist" and "olog"

N_ic – the frequency of the stem with the affix  _-ic_

N_ical – the frequency of the stem with the affix _-ical_

P_ical – the proportion of occurrences with the stem _-ic_


The data are based on the following CQP query ("hw" stands for the lemma in the BNC)

[word=".*(log|ist)ic(al)?" & pos=".*AJ.*"]
count Last by hw
