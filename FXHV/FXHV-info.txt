Co-occurrence frequencies of the collocational framework [a _ of] and its collocates in the Written Academic subsection of the British National Corpus Baby edition.


Description of the columns:

Token – the token in the written academic section of the BNC-BABY occurring in the slot provided by the collocational framework 

InPattern – the frequency of the token in the collocatonal framework

InRest –  the frequency of the token outside of the framework

c – the frequency of other tokens in the collocational framework

d – the frequency of other tokens outside of the framework

GStat – the G statistic of the log-likelihood ratio test

Frequency – whether the word is "more" or "less" frequent than expected


The data are based on the following CQP query:

[word="a"%c] [] [word="of"%c] :: match.text_genre="W:ac.*"
