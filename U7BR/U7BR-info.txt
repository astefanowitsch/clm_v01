Annotated frequency list of words containing the suffixes _-ic_ and _-ical_ in the LOB corpus.


Description of the columns:


Word – the word containing the suffix _-ic_ or _-ical_

Suffix – the suffix, with the values "ic" and "ical"

Stem – the stem, with hyphens removed, where they are present

StemLength – the length of the stem in number of characters

Frequency – the frequency of occurrence of the word in the corpus
