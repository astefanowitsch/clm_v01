Concordance of ADJ-NOUN sequences with _hot_, _warm_, _cool_, _cold_ in the British National Corpus, Baby Edition, annotated for metaphoricity and type of metaphor.


Description of the columns:

TokenID – the token position of the adjective in the corpus

FileID – the BNC-BABY file from which the example is drawn

SemCat – whether the (potential) adjective token is "metaph(orical)", "lit(eral)" or a "false" hit

MetCat – the metaphorical category of the adjective, as described in the book

Adj – the adjective

Noun – the noun

Example – the corpus example


The data are based on the following CQP query:

[word="(hot|warm|cool|cold)"%c & pos="AJ.*"][pos="NN.*"];
