Frequency of words (and other tokens) in the 2001 election manifestos of the British parties Labour and Liberal Debocrats.


Description of the columns:

Token – the token

FreqLabour – frequency of the token in the Labour manifesto

FreqLibdem – frequency of the token in the Liberal Democrats manifesto

c – frequency of other words

d – frequency of other words

GStat – the G statistic of the log-likelihood ratio test

Association – whether the token is more frequent in the "labour" or "libdem" manifesto


The data are based on the following CQP query (the empty brackets will match all tokens):

[]
count Last by word%c
